# FoS IPTV Privacy Notice

FoS IPTV is designed to work locally on your computer.

FoS IPTV may store source names, imported metadata, favorites, recently watched items, continue-watching progress, settings, and encrypted Xtream password data when the operating system keychain is available.

FoS IPTV does not provide IPTV channels, movies, series, playlists, provider accounts, credentials, or subscriptions.

Do not post your provider URL, username, password, or playlist publicly when requesting support.

FoS IPTV v0.1.0 does not include third-party analytics.
